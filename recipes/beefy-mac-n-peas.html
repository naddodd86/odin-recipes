Beefy Mac N Peas

A family favorite

Parents and kids alike love this twist on an intercontinental classic!

By adding some extra protein and veggies this becomes a hearty, tasty and quick meal to make for yourself or your family.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 boxes of Annie's Organic Mac and Cheese
  • 1 pound of lean ground beefy
  • 1 bag of flash-frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water in a medium saucepan to boil.
  2. While water is boiling add ground beef to skillet and cook until browned.
  3. Add pasta from box to saucepan and cook until desired tenderness.
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain in colander and set aside.
  5. Add cheese mix, milk and butter from box browned ground beef and stir thoroughly.
  6. Add pasta and frozen peas to mixture and heat until cheese is melted.
  7. Season to taste and serve!
